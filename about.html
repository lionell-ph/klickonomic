About Us

Founded in May 2025, Klickonomic is an innovative community marketplace system created to transform local trade and communication within barangay communities. Established by Rhys Cruz, Jayson Gilbert Cruz, Calvin Josh Cordova, Harry Caudilla, Lionell Cunanan, and Lauryn Evangelista, Klickonomic offers a single online platform where residents can buy, sell, barter, and stay informed about community events and concerns.

Our hybrid business model merges a commission-based marketplace with a freemium community portal. While the core features are free for everyone, local businesses and advertisers can access premium tools for greater visibility and outreach.



Our Mission

To empower barangay communities by providing a trusted, user-friendly digital platform that facilitates local trade, fosters communication, and strengthens social bonds which makes everyday life more convenient, connected, and collaborative.



Our Vision

To become the central digital hub of every barangay in the Philippines—seamlessly integrating commerce, communication, and community engagement through a sustainable and socially inclusive marketplace system.
